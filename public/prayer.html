PRAYER OF THE MONTH

Dear Lord God: I thank you for my church, for those who worship next to me each Sunday. Thank you for giving me these loving souls to walk alongside me on my journey through life. They aren't perfect Lord, but neither am I. Together we acknowledge our weaknesses and take hold of your strength. Together we are strong because you rise up stron among us each time we gather.

Bless those you've called to minister to us. Keep truth and faithfulness ever before our eyes. I thank you for them.

Amen.
