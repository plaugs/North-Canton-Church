OUR MISSION

Our Mission at HCF is “Revealing Jesus through Building Relationships”

We recognize that people need to:

  • Believe in the Lord Jesus Christ as their Lord & Savior.
  • Belong to the body of believers, and
  • Become the person that the Lord has destined them to be.

We have developed a process called, “The Practice of Discipleship” by which we:

  • Shepherd people in the love, acceptance & forgiveness of Jesus Christ.
  • Equip people to be disciples of Christ.
  • Develop people to reach their maximum potential in Christ.

Through this process we believe:

  • The individual will mature.
  • The family will be strengthened, and
  • The Church will be envisioned and empowered to fulfill their God-given ministry to influence & impact our communities, our nation, and the world around us.
